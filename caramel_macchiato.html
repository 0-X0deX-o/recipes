Caramel Macchiato

This is a recipe of my very own Caramel Macchiato. This recipe calls for a little bit of a homemade feel with the classic italian recipe that is pivotal to a traditional italian cafe with a modern twist.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Ground espresso
  • sugar free caramel syrup
  • cinnamon
  • frothed milk
  • nitro cream

Steps:

    pull the espresso shot and while it is brewing...
  1. froth/steam high quality milk
  2. add caramel syrup and cinnamon to the bottom of a cup
    after the espresso shot has been brewed...
  1. add the espresso shot to the cup
  2. pour in the frothed/steamed milk
  3. add the nitro cream on top
